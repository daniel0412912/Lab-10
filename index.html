SEO Techniques

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is important because it helps improve a website's visibility and search ranking. One SEO technique that is really effective, is keyword research and optimization. Which works by identifying keywords that are related to your website such as business keywords and displaying them in your website content. This makes it so search engines can understand your page easier through search queries (Hubspot).

Another SEO technique that is important for websites is backlink building. This involves taking links from other authoitative and well known websites and displaying them on your own website. The purpose is that high-quality backlinks essentially tell search engines that your site is trustworthy, which in turn improves its ranking (Mailchimp).

The third SEO technique is optimizing mobile responsiveness. Since the number of mobile users have grown, adding mobile responsiveness can increase SEO as a result increase website traffic. This is because search engines like Google prioritize mobile-friendly websites in their rankings, making them list higher in the search results (SEMrush).

SMO Techniques

Social Media Optimization (SMO) is also very important for boosting a website's online presence. One useful SMO technique is integrating social media sharing buttons on your webpage, such as a Facebook like buttom or instagram follow button. The addition of these buttons encourages users to interact with your content and increases the chances of them sharing it, expanding the reach of your website (Designer Websites).

Another SMO tactic that is important is creating shareable content. Content that will catch a users eye through images or videos is really effective. Since they can easily be shared across various social media platforms with a click of a button. The use of engaging content increases your brand visibility and user interaction as more people will be exposed to the content (American Library Association).

Lastly, adding relevant keywords to your social media profiles or within the content itself, such as contact details or unique watermarks can improve your social media presence and make it easier for users to find your content and engage with you (WebFX).
